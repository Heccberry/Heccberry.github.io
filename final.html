Welcome to my final project!

Hello, my name is Brandon Tran and welcome to my final CSC 106 project:
Ravenous Winston: The Anime, The Movie, The Game!

For this game I decided to create what is essentially snake but with a mild twist in tone.
Rather than play as a growing snake that eats fruit, you play as an incredibly angry Winston who's bent on chasing
down Oh Noes guy, cloning himself in the process and becoming an ever-increasing conga line of angry Winstons. Scary, huh?
The game also includes a built in tutorial screen that shows you the controls and instructions and even has a
lore button that takes you through a series of "cutscenes" that describe the comedic backstory behind Ravenous Winston.
